Who We Are

About Us

Our Story

Our story started in a basement in Racine, Wisconsin. We met in high school and became good friends right away, bonding over our shared love of MMA and the fact we were born at the same time in the same city. What began as a simple idea between two fans has grown into the business we run today. With hard work, support from the MMA community, and backing from Ben Rothwell, we’ve been able to turn our passion into something real. No matter how much we grow, we’ll always remember where we started and the people who helped us get here.

Meet the Team

Jack Rosenthal
Founder & Head of Management

Jack Rosenthal has always had an inclination for entrepreneurship and left high school early to develop his skills in business. Before entering the world of MMA, he held stints as a Christmas tree salesman, celebrity bodyguard, school mascot, and parade balloon handler. Jack became a credentialed journalist at 16 and has since covered some of the most exclusive events on the planet. He plans on becoming an Army combat correspondent.

Dorian Schneider
Co-Founder & Director of Talent

Dorian Schneider gave up school to pursue his love of MMA full time, moving alone to Wisconsin to train with the famed Ben Rothwell. He is very passionate about the craft of fighting and started competing in tournaments after just four weeks. In addition to Ben Rothwell, he has also trained with people like Beneil Dariush. It is Dorian’s goal to enter the United Fighting League (UFC).

Ben “Big Ben” Rothwell
Board Member & Partner
Head Coach, Rothwell MMA • Kenosha, WI

A Kenosha, Wisconsin native, Ben Rothwell is one of the most accomplished heavyweight fighters in MMA history. With a professional record of 39–14 and 28 knockouts, Rothwell competed in the UFC for over a decade, defeating top heavyweights including Alistair Overeem, Josh Barnett, and Matt Mitrione. In 2025, he captured the BKFC Heavyweight Championship at KnuckleMania 5. Off the canvas, Rothwell owns and operates Rothwell MMA in Kenosha — a premier martial arts academy that has trained fighters at every level since 2011 and is where several BAM roster members hone their craft.
